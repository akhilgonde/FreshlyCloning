How The Gift Works

You choose the plan size

Your gift starts their subscription on a free note by covering your recipient’s first delivery of fresh meals.

They take it from there

Your recipient will pick their first box of meals and set up their deliveries.

We cook and deliver

We cook their meals and send them directly to their door. After this first box, they can skip deliveries or cancel their subscription anytime.
